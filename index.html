About this webpage

As days went by, whenever my Dad came into my study room and saw me using the computer, he asked me what I was doing and I always responded "Programming". It continued like this for about a week until he then asked me in detail what sort of prgramming I was doing, for which I responded Web Development. He then challenged me to build a Website for the Family. Its just turns out that we were asked to build a family tree, so I decided to embed th Tree in the webpage. My future plan is to develop a platform similar to this, just for family information. More functions will be implemented, like accounts for each family members, in which they will be able to post photos, videos and achievements. It would be like a social media. Family-heads will have overall control of their family members and would be able to add or delete their contents. Power and conrol control would be in a hierarchical order from Grand parents to parents and grandchildren. This means, grand parents would be able to edit the contents of any family under their bloodline.
